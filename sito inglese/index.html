what is social media?

Social media has become incredibly prevalent in today's world. According to Datareportal, they are utilized by a staggering 61.4% of the global population, accounting for 4.95 billion people. Undoubtedly, the significance of this topic cannot be overstated, especially with Facebook retaining its position as the most widely used

social media platform. While social media has bestowed numerous benefits, such as fostering global connectivity and enabling instant communication and news sharing, it also comes with its fair share of drawbacks. One concern is the algorithm's ability to construct personalized profiles based on user behavior, predicting and influencing our actions. Additionally, social media addiction is a growing issue, with approximately 10% of Americans reportedly addicted, according to Addiction Help . Shockingly, this number skyrockets to 40% among teenagers.
